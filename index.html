Minsky Duo

A violin and piano duo

Welcome to our corner of the world

Minsky Duo's namesake comes from Marvin Minsky, a pioneer in the field of Artificial Intelligence, and a musician of unusual ability. It's our mission to celebrate such extraordinary musical minds by presenting their works for solo piano, solo violin and the works for violin & piano combined.

Members

Our members are Nato on violin and Monica Chew on piano.
